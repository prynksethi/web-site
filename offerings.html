Our services are designed with a focus of Start-Ups. In last few years we have realized the specific painpoints of start-ups when it comes to Design and Innovation. Entrepreneurs today know that Design is as important as having ech capability and a good Business model. Our current offerings are tailored to aid start-ups at different points in their journey.

SERVICE INNOVATION

Designing and Prototyping of every element of the service and the customer experiences that define it.

It results in a seamless experience for all the business partners, stakeholders and customers.

DESIGN SPRINT

Design Sprint is a 2-3 weeks process for solving a major Business problem through Design and Prototyping.

It results in the improvement of the experience of an interface or any other touchpoint.

EXPERT REVIEW

Expert Reviews help Businesses to identify why they are not getting the aspired customer reaction to a particular offering.

The result can be a new design direction or a design recommendation sheet.

TINKERFORM DESIGN WORKSHOPS

Design workshops are a means to propogate design learning and build an awareness about Design in India.

We hope to make people sensitized towards Design and Innovation with these workshops.
